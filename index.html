Microsoft News

Trusted News from the World’s Best Journalists

What is Microsoft News?

Microsoft News delivers high-quality news from the world’s most popular and trusted publishers, across web and mobile experiences for Microsoft as well as a growing number of syndication partners. At nearly a half a billion monthly users, our service reaches one of the largest audiences of news readers in the world. We are committed to delivering informative and entertaining content in an easily accessible, comprehensive and trustworthy way.

At Microsoft News we believe

A free, well-funded press plays a critical role in society

We’re committed to providing a sustainable source of revenue and growth for our publishing partners so they can focus on what they do best – producing high-quality journalism for the betterment of society.

In delivering unparalleled breadth and depth of high-quality journalism

We partner with more than 1,200 premium publishers that represent over 4,500 of the world’s most popular brands across all major markets to deliver the very best content available, free to everyone every day.

That trustworthy and diverse perspectives matter

It can be challenging and time-consuming to find news you can trust. People want to be better informed and they value multiple perspectives. Our partners include some of the most respected and trusted publishers, and together we can deliver a global view that focuses on the facts and seek to spread knowledge and understanding.

User choice and personalization are essential to the experience

Innovative technology and design allow us to put the reader in the driver’s seat – choosing the layout, sources, topics and stories that interests them the most. Our powerful AI and machine-learning systems constantly optimize and tunes the content to each person’s preferences.

Global Reach

Microsoft News reaches one of the largest audiences of news consumers in the world, nearly half a billion people in more than 140 countries and 28 languages.
